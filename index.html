Here's a quick guide to get you started

Add

Tap the Add tab to enter each medication. You'll need the name, dose, and your script details.

🏠
Today

Each morning, this screen shows all your medications. Tap each time slot as you take them.

📅
Doses

Track morning, midday, evening and night doses — or mark Unsure if you can't remember.

🔁
Repeats

Log each time you collect a repeat prescription. The app tracks how many you have left.

👁️
Carer

Share a quick snapshot of today's medications with a family member or carer — one tap to send.

📋
Scripts

View all your prescriptions, expiry dates, and repeats remaining in one place.

💾 All your data stays on this device — nothing is sent anywhere.
